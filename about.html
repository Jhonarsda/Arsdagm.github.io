About

Very complicated and dizzying warship world game.

In the midst of a fierce war on a thrilling sea, this warship is the main weapon. With its sophisticated weapons, this ship is a threat that cannot be underestimated. The sailors had to remain alert, because their every move could be a fateful decision. In a tense battle, courage is key. This ship is an invincible fortress, ready to face storms of cannon and fierce waves. Victory is the ultimate goal, and everyone must unite to achieve it. There is no room for fear on this ship, only strong determination and a fierce fighting spirit.

  • Ships fighting at sea.
  • one island map is filled with battles between ships.
  • large sea vehicles have weapons.
